Puzzle Description:
There is an unprotected terminal connected to the door with damaged circuitry, and the missing section can be translated into a logic gate format. A NOT gate has an input of 1, and a NAND gate has an input of 1 and 0. Their respective outputs are plugged into a XOR gate. The XOR output is then entered in alongside a 1 value into a AND gate. What is the final numerical output?
Puzzle Type:
Input
Puzzle Solution:
1
Puzzle Hint:
XOR gates only output 1 when their inputs are both 1
