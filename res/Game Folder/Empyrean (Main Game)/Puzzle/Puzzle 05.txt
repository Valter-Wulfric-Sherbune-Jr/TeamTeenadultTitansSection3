Puzzle Description:
There is exposed door circuitry with an incomplete area, and the missing section can be translated into a logic gate format. Two AND gates are present with one having inputs 1 and 0 while the other has inputs of 1 and 1. Both those AND gate outputs are then inputted into an OR gate. What is the final numerical output?
Puzzle Type:
Input
Puzzle Solution:
1
Puzzle Hint:
AND gates only output 1 if both inputs are also 1.
