Puzzle Description:
There is exposed door circuitry with an unfinished area, and the missing section can be translated into a logic gate format. An AND gate and a NOR gate both have inputs of 1 and 0. The outputs from those gates act as inputs for an OR gate, and that OR gates output is then recorded into a NOT gate. What is the final numerical output?
Puzzle Type:
Input
Puzzle Solution:
1
Puzzle Hint:
NOR gates only output 1 if both inputs are 0.
